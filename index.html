You are the spring guardian and your mission is to perform a ritual to summon spring that consists in making sure that all the map is green as Switzerland.

How To Play:

  • WASD or Arrow Keys to move.
  • Space to grab and release stuff.
  • Mouse to control position of elementals when grabbing a control staff.
  • V to mute audio.
  • N and B to increase or decrease volume.

Tech Used:

  • C++
  • Raylib
  • Emscripten Compiler (for web build)
  • Pinta (linux)
  • Audacity (Audio modifications)
  • sfxr.me (Sounds)
